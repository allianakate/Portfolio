About the Institution

The Philippine School For Technology Development & Innovation Inc. was incorporated in 2020 in Valenzuela City. Its creation was in response to the need to increase the training delivery capacity of the government led and managed by TESDA. The Institution initially focuses on the demand for skills and technical vocational education of the CAMANAVA area (Caloocan, Malabon, Navotas and Valenzuela) - a northern district of the National Capital Region with over 2,000,000 population, approximately 50,000 unemployed and 75,000 underemployed persons.

Vision

To be a reliable partner of the government in the provision of technical-vocational education and skills development in the National Capital Region.

Mission

To provide relevant and timely quality technical vocational development interventions to the youth in particular and the Filipino workforce in general to improve their employability and income.

Core Values

We believe that the quality of training will in the end spell the difference between success and failure in the labor market.
